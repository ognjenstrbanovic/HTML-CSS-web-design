Maximum Temperature

As one would expect, the weather becomes significantly warmer as we approach the equator (which is zero degrees latitude). What is more interesting, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer than the northern hemisphere at this time of the year. This may be because of the tilt of the earth at the time that this information was gathered.
